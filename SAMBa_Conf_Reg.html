Deadline

The deadline for meal pre-orders is 19th June 2019; after this date we will be unable to take further food orders. The deadline for registration is Friday 29th June. Anyone wishing to register who has not already done so will need to contact one of the organisers.
Students: The deadline for title and abstract submission for talks or posters is Wednesday 29th May.
Conference participants are expected to follow the code of conduct, available here.

Conference Meal

There will be an evening meal held on Monday 8th July. The venue will be Las Iguanas in Bath town centre.
All conference attendees are welcome, however we are unable to cover the costs of an individual's meal, so it will be at personal expense. If you are interested in coming please indicate when you complete the registration form.
Las Iguanas have asked us to pre-order our food; please complete the seperate evening meal pre-order form to confirm your order. You can view the menu here.
One you have submitted your pre-order, please:

  • Please contact Will Graham (email) to discuss payment options.
  • If you are based in Bath and would like to pay by cash, please hand your payment to one of the conference organisers who can be found in:
    • 4W2.21 (Lizzi Pitt, Will Graham)
    • 6W1.27 (Shaunagh Downing)
Please ensure you have paid before 12:00, Friday 5th July.
