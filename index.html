My undergraduate degree and previous education was an integrated masters MSci Mathematics and Physics at the University of Bath.

More information can be found here, or on my profile on LinkedIn.

Research Area

My research concerns the analysis of differential operators on functions defined on domains with empty interiors ("singular structures") and the analysis of their spectra. Such problems are motivated by a desire to understand how wave-guidance in thin waveguides is affected by the geometry of the waveguides design.
