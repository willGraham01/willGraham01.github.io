Research

My PhD research focuses on the analysis of differential systems on singular structure domains. Such structures serve as an approximation to thin waveguide structures in photonic and phononic applications. The ability to solve these approximate systems yeilds insights into how the geometry/ topology of the domain affects wave guidence within the structure.

I am supervised by Dr. Kirill Cherednichenko (80%), Prof. David Bird (10%), and Prof. Chris Bowen (10%).

I am supported by a scholarship from the EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training in Statistical Applied Mathematics at Bath (SAMBa), under the project EP/L015684/1.
