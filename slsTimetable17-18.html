National Physical Laboratory Visit Shedule

National Physical Laboratory visited us on the 1st of November. Resources from the presentation of the problems can be found on the SLS website.

  • 12:15-13:05 1W2.102: Informal Lunch with representatives from NPL. Refreshments will be provided.
  • 13:15-14:15 CB3.5: Presentation of the first set of industrial problems; plus a general introduction to NPL. Each problem will be followed by a short discussion.
  • 14:15-14:45 CB3.5: (FREE!) Coffee break.
  • 14:45-15:45 CB3.5: Presentation of the remaining problems, followed again by discussion.
  • 15:45-16:00 CB3.5: Time for general questions. This may turn into contingency time on the day.
